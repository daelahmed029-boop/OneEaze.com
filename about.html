About OneEase

Revolutionizing daily life through seamless digital integration

Our Mission

To simplify everyday life by integrating essential services into one seamless, secure, and intelligent super app platform.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's leading super app, empowering millions with convenient, connected digital experiences.

Our Story

OneEase was born from a simple observation: people were spending too much time switching between different apps for their daily needs. Shopping on Daraz, ordering food from Foodpanda, paying bills through Easypaisa - each requiring separate accounts, payments, and mental energy.

We asked: What if all these services could be seamlessly integrated into one platform? What if you could shop, eat, and pay without ever leaving a single app?

That vision became OneEase - Pakistan's first true super app that brings together e-commerce, food delivery, and digital payments into one unified experience.
